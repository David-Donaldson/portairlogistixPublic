Company Organization

PortAir Logistics Group Company Limited was founded on some core principles; professionalism, reliability, and a great commitment to creating customer-focused value-added services and best-in-class customer experience. We will bring to market a variety of products and services that will revolutionize the logistics space in Jamaica. We intend to bring to the Jamaican economy a reliable import/export carrier and partner that will be able to drive economic growth by offering consistent service.

Through diversified product offerings, we intend to make an impact by creating a relatable brand that plays an important role in community development as well as raising current standards.

Vision

To make a positive impact on our Local, Pan-Caribbean, and Global Economy by becoming a major global logistics company by delivering value to our customers through innovation, as well as creating sustainable solutions that will drive economic growth and a create a positive customer experience.

Mission
